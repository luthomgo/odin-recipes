Cottage Pie

Description

A budget friendly meal that looks, but also tastes good.

Ingredients

  • butter
  • mushroom
  • onions
  • celery
  • garlic
  • tomato paste
  • pasta
  • water
  • ground beef
  • salt
  • parsley
  • spices
  • cottage cheese
  • parmesan cheese
  • mozzarella

Steps

  1. Saute mushrooms, onions, parsley, celery and garlic in a large skillet with the butter
  2. Combine tomato paste, water, macaroni, salt sweetener and vegetables
  3. Add the ground beef and spices of your liking.
  4. Put ½ of this mixture in the bottom of crockpot.
  5. Top with 1 cup cottage cheese, 1 cup mozzarella and ½ of the Parmesan cheese. Repeat Layers.
  6. Cover and cook on low for 3-4 hours or high for 1 hour
