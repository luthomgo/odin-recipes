Pancakes

Description

Fluffy, soft pancake made from scratch using simple ingredients

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Egg
  • milk
  • Vanilla essence

Steps

  1. Melt the butter and set it aside.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla essence
  4. Create a well in the centre of your dry ingredients
  5. Pour in the milk mixture and stir gently with a fork until the flour is just mixed together
  6. Place a large skillet over medium heat. Sprinkle in a few drop of water to test if it's ready.
  7. Brush the skillet with melted butter
  8. Scoop the batter onto the skillet using a 1/4 cup measure or large cookie scoop and spread around
  9. After 1 or 2 minutes, the edge will look dry and bubble will form and pop on the surface.
  10. Flip the pancake and cook for another 1-2 minutes until golden brown
  11. Serve immediately with butter and mix berries
